Revisal

With great communication comes great work.

Freelancing often means juggling client feedback across emails, messaging apps, cloud storage, and multiple file versions. Our team saw an opportunity to simplify that experience by creating Revisal, a project management platform built specifically for creative professionals. Developed as our capstone project in collaboration with Sanjana Mittal, Jaspreet Kaur, Danice Biano, Rocel De Leon, Evan James Otilla, and Faribah Nawar, Revisal brings communication, project tracking, and file feedback into one intuitive workspace. As the UX/UI Designer, I contributed to user research, heuristic evaluation, wireframing, interface design, and usability testing to create a seamless experience that helps creatives spend less time managing projects and more time doing what they love.
