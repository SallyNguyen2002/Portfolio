Halloween Poster

One spooky poster?

Created as an in-class design challenge, this Halloween party poster was completed in just two hours from concept to final design. The highlight of the project is the DJ pumpkin, which I illustrated entirely by hand during class. Working under a tight deadline pushed me to make quick design decisions while balancing typography, illustration, and composition to create a bold, energetic poster that captures the excitement of a Halloween celebration.
