Awwwards

The best website for website inspiration. But is it the best website?

Awwwards is one of my favorite places to fall down a design rabbit hole. It is packed with bold ideas, beautiful interactions, and enough inspiration to keep my Figma tabs multiplying. But even the coolest websites have room to grow. So... who am I to critique Awwwards? Honestly, just a curious designer asking "What if?" This project is not about fixing the website. It is about exploring how a few UX improvements could make an already inspiring experience even smoother. Whether my ideas actually make it better, I'll let you be the judge.
