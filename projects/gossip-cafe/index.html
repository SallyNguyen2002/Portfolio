Gossip Cafe

Coffee deserves a little more personality.

Inspired by Calgary's bubble tea scene, this branding project reimagines the coffee shop experience through a more playful lens. I wanted to create an identity that captures the warmth of specialty coffee while embracing the colorful, energetic atmosphere that makes milk tea cafés so memorable.
