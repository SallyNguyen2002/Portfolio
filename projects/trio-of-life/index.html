Trio of Life

Human story in geometric.

There was a time when I was fascinated by geometric tattoo art:the precision, symmetry, and hidden symbolism behind every line. That interest inspired Trio of Life, a conceptual project that explores the cycle of birth, life, and death through minimalist geometric forms. Rather than using detailed illustrations, I challenged myself to communicate emotion and meaning using only simple shapes, balance, and composition.
