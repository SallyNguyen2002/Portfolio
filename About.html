About Me

I’m a UX design student passionate about creating impactful and aesthetically pleasing visuals. My design style leans towards clean, minimalistic, yet realistic elements. I believe that simple words and ideas can convey powerful stories. My inspirations mostly revolve around growth, self-reflection, and spiritual connection. My goal is to explore the world in a way that feels personal and resonant, while also appealing to others who share an appreciation for art that’s both meaningful and visually refined.
